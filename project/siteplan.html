WDD 131 Project Site Plan | Wyo Auto Slavage

Name

Wyo Auto Slavage

becuse it is for an auto salvage in wyoming

Purpose

To provide a gallery of used vehicles for sale, a form to see if we have the part you need and, a form to request a estament value for your damaged vehicle.

Scenarios

Scenario 1#

Can I contact this company and talk with someone?

To help with this the footer will contain contact info

Scenario 2#

Is this company local? Can I stop by and pick up my part?

To help with this the footer will contain the localtion as part of the contact info

Color Schema

The colors will be blue and orange. The navbar, major headers and, footers will be blue. The form borders, buttons, photo&info cards backgrounds, and minor headers will be orange.

Typography

headers, footer and nav tags will uses Inter while all other text will use Roboto
